Who I Am

Committed to the CUSA Mission

I am miskaspiritualyti — a CUSA service member with a deep passion for structured gameplay, community building, and meaningful leadership. Over the past two years, I have dedicated myself to multiple branches and departments within Clockwork's United States Army, earning respect and trust at every level.

My journey began in the United States Marine Corps, where I learned discipline and tactical fundamentals. From there, I expanded into protective services, naval command, judicial authority, and ultimately community moderation — each step strengthening my skills and broadening my perspective on what it means to serve.

I believe in leading by example: communicating clearly, helping others grow, and maintaining patience even in high-pressure situations. Whether I'm commanding a fleet, presiding as a Federal Judge, or moderating the community, my goal is always the same — to make CUSA a better place for everyone who serves.
